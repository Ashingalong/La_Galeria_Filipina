The Artists

This year's exposition brings together a remarkable ensemble of artists whose works span medium, era, and intention — united by a commitment to craft and authentic expression.

Maria Santos
Oil Painting · Mixed Media
Known for her earthy palettes and introspective compositions, Maria explores themes of memory and place through layered oil techniques.

Submit Your Work to the Expo

We welcome submissions from all artists — student, emerging, and established. Works are reviewed by our curatorial panel for appropriateness, quality, and thematic fit before being featured in the online and physical gallery.

  • All mediums accepted: painting, photography, sculpture, digital, video
  • Images must be minimum 2000px on the shortest side, in JPG or PNG
  • Videos must be uploaded as MP4 or MOV, maximum 500 MB
  • Submissions are reviewed within 5–7 business days
  • Content must be original work by the submitting artist
  • Works containing inappropriate or offensive content will not be accepted
